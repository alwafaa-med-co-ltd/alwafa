The nature of Al-Wafa'a Medical Co., Ltd activities is trading in all kinds of finished medicines, medical appliances, and laboratory equipment or reagents. The Company is prepared to furnish all hospitals and clinics with state-of-the-art medical furniture, or as per customers' requirements.

Serving the Market

We are serving the market through selling and distributing finished medicines and medical appliances to those who require them in the public sector and the private sector. This is done through our branches and subagents all around the territory.

The Regular Flow of Supply

Al-Wafa'a Medical Co., Ltd has worked very hard to keep the supply of our product range continuously to the private sector and to the Government of Yemen through tenders' announcements to meet the public hospitals requirements.
